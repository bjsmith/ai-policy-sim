## United States

The United States leads global AI investment with overwhelming private sector participation. [US private AI investment reached $109.1 billion in 2024, nearly 12 times China's $9.3 billion](https://www.secondtalent.com/resources/usa-vs-china-ai-llm-statistics/). Cumulative private investment between 2013-2024 totaled [$471 billion in the US compared to China's $119 billion](https://www.recordedfuture.com/research/measuring-the-us-china-ai-gap).

Individual companies attract massive capital: [OpenAI, Anthropic, and xAI alone captured over $50 billion combined through August 2025](https://www.wizanetech.com/2025/11/the-global-ai-investment-race-why-us-is_25.html). [AI deal value in the US already exceeded 2024 levels by 61.5% to reach $174.6 billion](https://business20channel.tv/ai-investments-by-country-2025-statistics-funds-companies-trends-12-december-2024) by mid-2025. Federal R&D spending also increased from [$8.2 billion in 2021 to $10.4 billion in 2024](https://www.ai-hive.net/post/comparative-analysis-of-us-and-china-ai-infrastructure-and-development-a-2025-perspective).

**Model Parameters:** Starting investment of $109B annually, 30% annual growth rate reflecting venture capital boom and continued private sector enthusiasm, 90% constraint factor representing some market efficiency limits but minimal policy interference.
